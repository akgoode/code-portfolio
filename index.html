About Me

I am a dependable and driven web developer looking to add my skills to a team in a new and exciting workplace. While I was leading camping trips a few years ago, the most important thing we taught clients is to always leave the campsite a little better than you found it, and that is the approach I take to every position I have ever occupied. From small changes in operations to big changes in the way people think about projects, I am always striving to make my coworkers and my workflows simpler and more efficient. With a varied background in several different fields that involved daily interaction with many different kinds of people, I know that I can integrate seamlessly into any environment in which I may find myself. My ideal job would be working for a team in an established company as a way to build up my experience in web development.

A full stack social media app where users can make posts and view others posts! The front-end was built with Ember and the back end uses Rails and a PostgreSQL database. This app is in its preliminary stages so check back often for more updates!
